ABOUT.

This page is all about me.

I currently study Computing Science at the University of Dundee. I have studied Computing Science form National 5 all the way up to Advanced Higher in school. I am very fond of the more physical aspects of Computing which I will be studying next semester. For my Advanced Higher project I made a remote control car that was powered by and Arduino Uno and controlled by a remote that was powered by and Arduino Nano. This project achieved 90/100 marks and I still managed to get an overall B grade.
